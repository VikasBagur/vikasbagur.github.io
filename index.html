About Me

Experienced data analyst with a proven track record of extracting actionable insights from complex datasets and prior experience in supply chain. I specialize in data extraction, data analysis, and visualizing data, with proficiency in SQL, Python, MS Excel, AWS, and various visualization tools.

As a data analyst, I have successfully created reports and dashboards for teams to track their activities and performance. I extracted data from multiple sources using SQL ETL pipelines and Python scripts to reduce delays in day-to-day activities. Prior to working in data analytics, I served as a product analyst in a SaaS solutions development company, building solutions for supply chain stakeholders.

I hold a master’s degree in Industrial and Systems Engineering from the University of Southern California, where I gained knowledge about supply chain, and a Professional Certificate in Data Analytics from Google.

I am committed to delivering high-quality results through meticulous data analysis and clear communication of findings. I enjoy collaborating with multidisciplinary teams and thrive in fast-paced, data-driven environments. I firmly believe in the power of lifelong learning and actively seek opportunities to enhance my expertise.
